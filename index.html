About Me

I enjoy traveling, scuba diving, and fitness. I also attend a variety of technology meetups to stay updated on Machine Learning and IoT. Coming from a consulting background, which focused on crafting products for customers, I prize user-driven development. When developing software, I keep the user in mind at every phase of development.

I'm particularly interested in designing scalable systems.  I enjoy immersing myself into challenging problems and strategically determining how to optimize them. I'm inspired by products that solve important problems and have a wide set of users and situations that they can be applied to.
